The professionals directory contains every healthcare professional active in Vitacore. Professionals are linked to document types, specialties, sedes, and consultorios â€” all of which must be configured before you can create a professional record.

## Prerequisites

Before registering a professional, confirm all of the following exist:

- At least one [document type](/configuration/document-types)
- At least one [specialty](/configuration/specialties)
- At least one [sede](/configuration/headquarters)
- At least one [office (consultorio)](/configuration/offices) in that sede

<Warning>
If any prerequisite catalog is empty, the corresponding dropdown on the professional registration form will be blank and you will not be able to save the record.
</Warning>

## Field reference

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `tipo_documento_id` | Yes | Document type used for identification. Selected from active document types. |
| `numero_documento` | Yes | Document number. Must be unique within the same document type. |
| `nombres` | Yes | Given name(s) of the professional. |
| `apellidos` | Yes | Family name(s) of the professional. |
| `especialidad_id` | Yes | The medical specialty assigned to this professional. |
| `sede_id` | Yes | The sede where the professional is based. |
| `consultorio_id` | Yes | The specific consultorio assigned to the professional. |
| `registro_profesional` | No | Professional license or registration number issued by the licensing authority. |
| `correo` | No | Professional email address. |
| `telefono` | No | Contact phone number. |
| `estado` | â€” | Status: `ACTIVO` or `INACTIVO`. Defaults to `ACTIVO` on creation. |

<Tip>
The `numero_documento` uniqueness constraint is scoped per `tipo_documento_id`. Two professionals can share the same number if they use different document types, though this is uncommon.
</Tip>

## Managing professionals

### Registering a professional

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm prerequisites">
    Verify that document types, specialties, sedes, and consultorios are all configured and have at least one active record each.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the new professional form">
    Navigate to **Configuration â†’ Professionals** and click **Nuevo profesional**, or go to `/hc/configuracion/profesionales/nuevo`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter identity information">
    Select the `tipo_documento_id` and enter the `numero_documento`. Fill in `nombres` and `apellidos`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Assign specialty and location">
    Select the `especialidad_id`, `sede_id`, and `consultorio_id`. The consultorio dropdown typically filters to show only offices belonging to the selected sede.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add credentials and contact details">
    Optionally enter `registro_profesional`, `correo`, and `telefono`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit the form. The professional is registered with status `ACTIVO`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing a professional

Click **Editar** in the professionals list or navigate to `/hc/configuracion/profesionales/editar/<item_id>`. All fields are editable, including the sede and consultorio assignment.

### Toggling status

Click the status badge to toggle between `ACTIVO` and `INACTIVO` via a `POST` to `/hc/configuracion/profesionales/toggle/<item_id>`.

<Note>
Deactivating a professional preserves all their historical records. They will no longer appear in active dropdowns for scheduling or assignment, but their past clinical records remain intact.
</Note>

---
title: "Services"
description: "Define the medical services your organization provides. Each service is linked to a specialty."
---

Medical services are the specific procedures or consultations your organization offers (e.g., General Consultation, Echocardiogram, Prenatal Control). Each service belongs to exactly one specialty.

<Note>
You must create at least one specialty before you can create a service. The specialty selector on the service form will be empty otherwise.
</Note>

## Field reference

| Field | Required | Description |
|---|---|---|
| `codigo` | No | Short code or identifier for the service (e.g., `CONS-GEN`). |
| `nombre` | Yes | Full name of the service. |
| `especialidad_id` | Yes | The specialty this service belongs to. Selected from active specialties. |
| `descripcion` | No | Optional description of what the service includes. |

<Note>
Services do not have an explicit `estado` toggle in the current implementation. To remove a service from active use, edit it and reassign or leave it unlinked from scheduling.
</Note>

## Routes

| Action | Method | URL |
|---|---|---|
| List all services | `GET` | `/hc/configuracion/servicios` |
| New service form | `GET` | `/hc/configuracion/servicios/nuevo` |
| Create service | `POST` | `/hc/configuracion/servicios/crear` |
| Edit service form | `GET` | `/hc/configuracion/servicios/<id>/editar` |
| Update service | `POST` | `/hc/configuracion/servicios/<id>/actualizar` |
| Toggle status | `POST` | `/hc/configuracion/servicios/toggle/<item_id>` |

<Tip>
Note that services use a two-step URL pattern: the form is at `/nuevo` while the create action posts to `/crear`. Similarly, the edit form is at `/<id>/editar` and the update action posts to `/<id>/actualizar`. This differs from other configuration modules.
</Tip>

## Managing services

### Creating a service

<Steps>
  <Step title="Confirm specialties exist">
    Go to **Configuration → Specialties** and verify at least one active specialty is listed. Without an active specialty, you cannot save a new service.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the new service form">
    Navigate to **Configuration → Services** and click **Nuevo servicio**, or go to `/hc/configuracion/servicios/nuevo`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill in the fields">
    Select the `especialidad_id` from the dropdown. Enter the service `nombre`. Optionally add a `codigo` and `descripcion`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save">
    Submit the form. The POST goes to `/hc/configuracion/servicios/crear`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Editing a service

Click **Editar** in the service list or navigate to `/hc/configuracion/servicios/<id>/editar`. Changes are submitted via `POST` to `/hc/configuracion/servicios/<id>/actualizar`.

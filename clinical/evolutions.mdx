## Overview

Clinical evolutions are the core documentation tool in Vitacore. Each evolution represents a single consultation visit and captures the structured narrative of the encounter — from the patient's complaint through to the physician's plan.

Evolutions are linked to a specific patient and are accessed from the patient detail view.

## Evolution fields

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| `motivo_consulta` | Chief complaint — why the patient is seeking care |
| `enfermedad_actual` | Current illness history — detailed description of the presenting condition |
| `examen_fisico` | Physical examination findings |
| `impresion_diagnostica` | Diagnostic impression — the working or confirmed diagnosis |
| `plan` | Treatment plan — medications, procedures, referrals, follow-up instructions |
| `medico` | Attending physician name |
| `paciente_id` | Patient this evolution belongs to (set automatically from the URL) |

## Routes

| Method | URL | Description |
|--------|-----|-------------|
| `GET` | `/hc/evoluciones/paciente/{paciente_id}` | List evolutions for a patient |
| `GET` | `/hc/evoluciones/nuevo/{paciente_id}` | New evolution form |
| `POST` | `/hc/evoluciones/crear/{paciente_id}` | Create evolution |

## Recording a new evolution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the patient detail view">
    Navigate to `/hc/pacientes/ver/{id}` for the patient you are documenting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Access the evolutions list">
    From the patient detail, navigate to `/hc/evoluciones/paciente/{paciente_id}` to see all previous consultation notes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the new evolution form">
    Navigate to `/hc/evoluciones/nuevo/{paciente_id}` to open the consultation documentation form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete the clinical note">
    Fill in the structured fields: chief complaint, current illness, physical exam, diagnostic impression, and treatment plan. Enter the attending physician's name.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save the evolution">
    Submit to `POST /hc/evoluciones/crear/{paciente_id}`. The `paciente_id` is taken from the URL — you do not select the patient in the form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the record">
    On success, you are redirected to the patient's evolution list. The new note appears at the top of the chronological history.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Clinical documentation guidance

<Tip>
Use the **motivo_consulta** field for the patient's own words describing why they came in. Reserve **enfermedad_actual** for the clinical narrative — onset, duration, associated symptoms, and relevant history.
</Tip>

<Tip>
The **impresion_diagnostica** field accepts free text. You may enter ICD codes alongside plain-language descriptions (e.g., "J06.9 — Acute upper respiratory infection").
</Tip>

<Note>
Evolutions are created but not edited. Each consultation generates a new, immutable record. If a correction is needed, create a new evolution that references and supersedes the previous one.
</Note>

## Viewing evolution history

The evolution list at `/hc/evoluciones/paciente/{paciente_id}` displays all recorded consultations for that patient in chronological order. Each entry shows the consultation data including the physician and the full clinical note.
